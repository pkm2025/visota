## 1. Personal Income Tax (Thuế TNCN) 2026

### Legal Basis
- **Law No. 109/2025/QH15** - Amended PIT Law, passed by National Assembly on Dec 10, 2025; effective **July 1, 2026** (salary/wage income rules apply from Jan 1, 2026)
- **Decree No. 253/2026/ND-CP** - PIT implementation guidance, effective **July 1, 2026**
- **Circular No. 87/2026/TT-BTC** - Detailed PIT implementation, issued June 30, 2026; effective **July 1, 2026**
- **Resolution 110/2025/UBTVQH15** - Resolution adjusting PIT exemption thresholds

### Key Deduction Amounts (effective from Jan 1, 2026)

| Deduction Type | 2025 Amount | 2026 Amount |
|---|---|---|
| Personal deduction (bản thân) | 11,000,000 VND/month | **15,500,000 VND/month** (186,000,000 VND/year) |
| Dependent deduction (người phụ thuộc) | 4,400,000 VND/month | **6,200,000 VND/month** |

### New PIT Progressive Tax Brackets (5 brackets, reduced from 7)

Effective from tax period 2026 (applied for salary/wage income from Jan 1, 2026):

| Bracket | Monthly Taxable Income (VND) | Annual Taxable Income (VND) | Tax Rate |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Up to 10,000,000 | Up to 120,000,000 | **5%** |
| 2 | Over 10,000,000 - 30,000,000 | Over 120M - 360M | **10%** |
| 3 | Over 30,000,000 - 50,000,000 | Over 360M - 600M | **20%** |
| 4 | Over 50,000,000 - 100,000,000 | Over 600M - 1,200M | **30%** |
| 5 | Over 100,000,000 | Over 1,200,000,000 | **35%** |

### Key Changes in Decree 253/2026/ND-CP (effective July 1, 2026)

1. **Non-taxable meal allowance**: Increased from 730,000 VND/month to **1,200,000 VND/month** (employer-provided meals remain fully exempt)
2. **Supplementary pension/life insurance**: Non-taxable cap increased from 1,000,000 VND to **3,000,000 VND/month**
3. **Dependent income threshold**: Increased from 1,000,000 VND to **3,000,000 VND/month** (a person qualifies as dependent if their average monthly income does not exceed this amount)
4. **10% withholding threshold**: Increased from 2,000,000 VND to **5,000,000 VND** per payment
5. **Annual PIT finalization exemption**: For secondary income subject to 10% withholding, exemption threshold raised from 10,000,000 to **15,000,000 VND/month** average
6. **New medical deduction**: Up to **23,000,000 VND/year** for eligible medical expenses
7. **New education deduction**: Up to **24,000,000 VND/year** for eligible education expenses
8. **5-year PIT exemption** for qualifying digital technology professionals (AI, semiconductors, R&D)
9. **Expanded dependent criteria**: Children over 18 in certain conditions may now qualify as dependents
10. **Severance/job-loss allowance**: Excess over statutory entitlement now PIT-exempt if documented in company policies
11. **Non-resident rate**: Flat **20%** on Vietnam-sourced salary/wage income

### Filing Deadlines
- **Employer finalization**: Last day of 3rd month after year-end (March 31)
- **Individual self-finalization**: Last day of 4th month after year-end (April 30)

## 2. Corporate Income Tax (Thuế TNDN) 2026

### Legal Basis
- **Law No. 67/2025/QH15** - Amended CIT Law (effective from fiscal year 2026)
- **Decree No. 320/2025/ND-CP** - CIT implementation guidance, issued Dec 15, 2025; effective **Dec 15, 2025**, applied from fiscal year 2026
- **Decree No. 141/2026/ND-CP** - Amendments to Decree 320/2025, issued April 29, 2026
- **Circular No. 20/2026/TT-BTC** - Detailed CIT guidance, issued March 2026

### CIT Rates Based on Enterprise Revenue (total revenue of preceding year)

| Enterprise Size | Annual Revenue Threshold | CIT Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Micro/super-small (siêu nhỏ) | Up to **3 billion VND/year** | **15%** |
| Small (nhỏ) | Over 3 billion - 50 billion VND/year | **17%** |
| Medium/Large (vừa và lớn) | Over 50 billion VND/year | **20%** |

### Key Provisions of Decree 141/2026/ND-CP
- **Tax exemption**: Enterprises (and household businesses) with annual revenue **up to 1 billion VND/year** are exempt from CIT
- Applied from January 1, 2026
- Revenue thresholds determine enterprise size classification based on preceding tax year

### Special CIT Rates
- **20%**: Standard rate for most enterprises (large enterprises)
- **17%**: Small and medium enterprises
- **15%**: Micro/small enterprises (revenue under 3 billion VND)
- Preferential rates (10%, 12%) apply to specific sectors/zones per investment incentives
- **Global Minimum Tax**: 15% effective rate for MNEs with consolidated revenue >= 750 million EUR (Pillar Two)

## 3. Electronic Invoices (Hóa đơn điện tử) 2026

### Legal Basis
- **Law No. 108/2025/QH15** - Law on Tax Administration 2025
- **Decree No. 254/2026/ND-CP** - E-invoice regulations, effective **July 1, 2026**
  - Replaces: Decree 123/2020/ND-CP, Decree 41/2022/ND-CP, Decree 70/2025/ND-CP
- **Circular No. 91/2026/TT-BTC** - E-invoice implementation guidance, issued June 30, 2026; effective **July 1, 2026**
  - Replaces: Circular 32/2025/TT-BTC

### Key Changes from Previous Regulations (vs. TT 78/2021 era)

1. **Replaces all prior e-invoice decrees** (NĐ 123/2020, NĐ 41/2022, NĐ 70/2025) with a unified framework
2. **Household business threshold**: Businesses with revenue up to **1 billion VND/year** are not required to use e-invoices (per NĐ 141/2026 changes)
3. **Invoice types maintained**: E-invoices with tax authority code (có mã) and without code (không có mã)
4. **Cash register-initiated e-invoices** (hóa đơn khởi tạo từ máy tính tiền): Clarified regulations; not mandatory if already using coded/uncoded invoices
5. **Electronic vouchers** (chứng từ điện tử): New comprehensive framework alongside e-invoices
6. **Invoice adjustment/replacement**: New procedures per Circular 91/2026
7. **Whistleblower rewards** (khen thưởng tố giác): New provisions for reporting invoice violations
8. **Invoice creation timing**: Clarified rules on when invoices must be issued

### E-Invoice Types Required Under NĐ 254/2026

| Invoice Type | Applicable To |
|---|---|
| E-invoice with tax authority code | Most enterprises; tax authority validates and issues code |
| E-invoice without tax authority code | Qualified enterprises meeting IT infrastructure requirements |
| Cash register e-invoice | Retail establishments (optional if already using other types) |
| Specific-sector e-invoices | Petroleum, real estate, aviation, insurance, etc. |

### Compliance Requirements for ERP Systems
- Must support both coded and uncoded e-invoice formats
- Must integrate with tax authority's e-invoice transmission system
- Must handle invoice adjustment, replacement, and cancellation workflows per TT 91/2026
- Must support household business exemption threshold (1 billion VND/year revenue check)

## 4. Social Insurance (BHXH) 2026

### Legal Basis
- **Law No. 41/2024/QH15** - Social Insurance Law 2024
- **Law on Health Insurance 2024**
- **Law on Employment 2025** (Luật Việc làm)
- **Decree No. 158/2025/ND-CP** - Amends Decree 58/2020/ND-CP on social insurance contributions
- **Decision 595/QD-BHXH** - Contribution rate regulations

### Contribution Rates (unchanged from 2025, per Law on Social Insurance 2024)

| Insurance Type | Employer (NSDLĐ) | Employee (NLĐ) | Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| Social Insurance (BHXH) - Total | **17.5%** | **8%** | 25.5% |
| -- of which: Sickness & maternity fund | 3% | 0% | 3% |
| -- of which: Retirement & survivor fund | 14.5% | 8% | 22.5% |
| Health Insurance (BHYT) | **3%** | **1.5%** | 4.5% |
| Unemployment Insurance (BHTN) | **1%** | **1%** | 2% |
| **Total (BHXH + BHYT + BHTN)** | **21.5%** | **10.5%** | **32%** |

### Additional Contributions

| Type | Employer | Employee |
|---|---|---|
| Labor Accident & Occupational Disease (BHTNLD & BNN) | **0.5%** | 0% |
| Trade Union Fee (KPCD) | **2%** | 0% |

### Salary Base for Insurance Contributions
- **Minimum**: Equal to the reference salary (mức tham chiếu) = **2,530,000 VND/month** (from July 1, 2026, per Decree 161/2026/ND-CP)
- **Maximum**: 20 times the reference salary = **50,600,000 VND/month** (increased from 46,800,000 VND)
- Reference salary increased from 2,340,000 VND to **2,530,000 VND/month** effective July 1, 2026

### Key Notes
- Rates remain **unchanged** from 2025; the Law on Social Insurance 2024 continues to govern
- The BHXH contribution is split: 3% to sickness/maternity fund (employer only), remaining to retirement/survivor fund
- Foreign workers in Vietnam: Subject to same BHXH rates as Vietnamese workers (under Law on Social Insurance 2024)

## 5. Minimum Wage by Region (Lương tối thiểu vùng) 2026

### Legal Basis
- **Decree No. 293/2025/ND-CP** - Minimum wage for employees under labor contracts, issued Nov 10, 2025; effective **January 1, 2026**
- Replaces: Decree No. 74/2024/ND-CP

### Monthly Minimum Wage Rates (effective January 1, 2026)

| Region | Monthly Minimum Wage (VND) | Increase from 2025 |
|---|---|---|
| Region I (Vùng I) | **5,310,000** | +350,000 |
| Region II (Vùng II) | **4,730,000** | +310,000 |
| Region III (Vùng III) | **4,140,000** | +270,000 |
| Region IV (Vùng IV) | **3,700,000** | +250,000 |

- Average increase: **7.2%** across all regions
- Applied to employees working under labor contracts per the Labor Code

### Hourly Minimum Wage

| Region | Hourly Rate (VND) |
|---|---|
| Region I | 25,600 |
| Region II | 22,800 |
| Region III | 19,900 |
| Region IV | 17,800 |

### Base Salary (Lương cơ sở) - Effective July 1, 2026
- Increased from 2,340,000 VND to **2,530,000 VND/month** (per Decree 161/2026/ND-CP)
- Affects pension and social insurance benefit calculations

### Regional Classification (34 provinces/cities)
- **Region I**: Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City (urban districts), and major urban areas
- **Region II**: Suburban districts of major cities, medium urban areas
- **Region III**: Provincial cities, smaller urban districts
- **Region IV**: All remaining areas (rural districts, smaller towns)

## 6. Value Added Tax (Thuế GTGT) 2026

### Legal Basis
- **Law on VAT 2024** (amended)
- **Resolution No. 204/2025/QH15** - VAT reduction policy
- **Decree No. 174/2025/ND-CP** - VAT reduction from 10% to 8%, effective **July 1, 2025 to December 31, 2026**

### Current VAT Rates (2026)

| Rate | Applicable Goods/Services |
|---|---|
| **0%** | Exported goods/services, certain international transport, goods/services in non-tariff zones |
| **5%** | Essential goods: clean water, food, educational materials, medical equipment, publications, social housing, agriculture inputs |
| **8%** (reduced from 10%) | Most goods/services eligible for the temporary reduction under Decree 174/2025 |
| **10%** (standard) | Goods/services NOT eligible for the 8% reduction (see exclusions below) |

### 8% Reduced VAT - Scope and Exclusions

**Eligible for 8%** (from July 1, 2025 to December 31, 2026):
- Most goods and services previously taxed at 10% receive a 2-percentage-point reduction to 8%

**NOT eligible (remain at 10%)**:
- Telecommunications, financial/banking services, insurance, securities
- Metal mining products (excluding coal)
- Products subject to special consumption tax (SCT)
- Real estate trading
- Certain goods at 5% VAT (no change for these)
- Passenger transportation by large buses (24+ seats)

### Key Implementation Notes
- The 8% reduction is **temporary**, ending **December 31, 2026**
- Invoices must clearly show the applied VAT rate
- A VAT rate appendix (Phụ lục giảm thuế GTGT) must be maintained
- Businesses must correctly classify goods/services into applicable rate categories

## 7. Household Business Tax Management (Quản lý thuế hộ kinh doanh) 2026

### Legal Basis
- **Decree No. 68/2026/ND-CP** - Tax policy for household businesses
- **Decree No. 141/2026/ND-CP** - Amends Decree 68/2026, issued April 29, 2026
- **Circular No. 18/2026/TT-BTC** - Tax administration for household businesses and individuals
- **Circular No. 50/2026/TT-BTC** - Amends Circular 18/2026, issued May 13, 2026; effective immediately
- **Law on PIT 2025** (No. 109/2025/QH15) and **Law on VAT 2024** (amended)

### Key Provisions

#### Revenue Threshold for Tax Obligation
- Household businesses with annual revenue **up to 1 billion VND/year**: **Exempt from CIT/PIT on business income** (per Decree 141/2026 amendment)
- Household businesses with annual revenue **over 1 billion VND/year**: Must declare and pay tax

#### Tax Method Change (effective January 1, 2026)
- **Previous**: Presumptive tax method (phương pháp thuế khoán) - lump-sum tax based on estimated revenue
- **New (2026)**: Transition to **declaration method** (phương pháp kê khai) - self-declaration and self-payment
  - Household businesses must now keep proper accounting records
  - File tax declarations using form 01/CNKD per Circular 50/2026/TT-BTC

#### VAT and PIT for Household Businesses
- **VAT threshold**: Revenue up to **500 million VND/year** = not subject to VAT (per amended VAT Law 2024)
- Revenue over 500 million VND/year: Must declare VAT (1% presumptive or declaration method)
- **PIT**: Household business income taxed as business income per PIT progressive brackets
- Must use e-invoices if revenue exceeds 1 billion VND/year (per NĐ 254/2026)

#### Circular 50/2026/TT-BTC Changes
- Updates and replaces 4 tax declaration forms for household businesses
- Requires bank account registration for tax payments
- New declaration forms and filing procedures
- Effective from May 13, 2026 (immediate upon signing)

#### Decree 141/2026/ND-CP Key Changes
- Raised revenue exemption threshold from previous level to **1 billion VND/year**
- Applied to both household businesses and micro/small enterprises
- Supplementary tax incentive mechanisms for SMEs

## ERP System Compliance Implications

### Must-Have Features for Visota ERP

1. **PIT Calculation Engine**:
   - 5-bracket progressive tax calculation (5%, 10%, 20%, 30%, 35%)
   - Personal deduction: 15,500,000 VND/month
   - Dependent deduction: 6,200,000 VND/month per dependent
   - New thresholds for non-taxable allowances (meal: 1.2M, insurance: 3M)
   - Medical/education deductions (23M/24M annual caps)
   - 10% withholding at 5M threshold
   - Dependent income threshold: 3M/month

2. **CIT Calculation**:
   - Revenue-based rate selection (15%/17%/20%)
   - Tax exemption handling for revenue <= 1 billion VND
   - Enterprise size classification logic

3. **E-Invoice Integration**:
   - Support for coded and uncoded e-invoices per NĐ 254/2026
   - Invoice adjustment/replacement per TT 91/2026
   - Cash register invoice support
   - Tax authority transmission integration

4. **Social Insurance Calculation**:
   - Employer rates: 21.5% total (BHXH 17.5% + BHYT 3% + BHTN 1%)
   - Employee rates: 10.5% total (BHXH 8% + BHYT 1.5% + BHTN 1%)
   - Labor accident insurance: 0.5% (employer)
   - Trade union fee: 2% (employer)
   - Salary ceiling: 50,600,000 VND/month (20x reference salary)
   - Reference salary: 2,530,000 VND/month

5. **Minimum Wage Compliance**:
   - Region-based minimum wage tables (4 regions)
   - Region I: 5,310,000 VND | Region II: 4,730,000 VND
   - Region III: 4,140,000 VND | Region IV: 3,700,000 VND

6. **VAT Handling**:
   - Support for 0%, 5%, 8%, 10% rates
   - 8% temporary reduction through Dec 31, 2026
   - Goods/services classification by applicable rate
   - VAT rate appendix generation

7. **Household Business Tax**:
   - Revenue threshold tracking (1 billion VND/year for CIT exemption)
   - VAT threshold tracking (500 million VND/year)
   - Declaration method support (not presumptive)
   - Form 01/CNKD per Circular 50/2026
